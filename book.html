The Story

You have read about the life of the Prophet ﷺ. But what happened after his departure?

How did the Sahaba carry the weight of Islam and show it to the world? And most importantly, what was the role of Hazrat Abu Bakr (RA), the Legend of our story?

How did the Sahaba manage to carry on without the guiding hand of the Prophet, deal with the enemies of Islam, and the enemies within their own ranks?

The Legend of As-Siddique is the first-ever simplified English biography of Hazrat Abu Bakr (RA) — the first book of The Sahaba Series, a compiled legacy that begins with the departure of the Prophet ﷺ and ends with the martyrdom of Hazrat Imam Hussain (AS).

In this first book, we discover the Companion of the Cave leading the Ummah, preventing them from going astray, and conquering the armies of evil:

  • The Ridda Wars
  • The Battle of the Apostates
  • The Compilation of the Qur'an
  • The Caliphate of Hazrat Abu Bakr (RA)
