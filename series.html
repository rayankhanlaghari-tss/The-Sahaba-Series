The Sahabas Series

Redefining Muslim Heroes.

A collection of narrative biographies bringing the lives of the Prophet's Companions to a new generation of readers.

The Sahabas Series began with a simple question: why don't more people know these stories the way they know other legends? Every companion carried a piece of the Prophet's ﷺ mission forward — through war, grief, and the leadership no one had trained them for. Those stories deserve to be told with the same weight and humanity as any epic, in language that doesn't feel like a history lecture.

Each volume follows one companion, in their own moment, facing their own trial. But together, they form a single unbroken thread.

The series begins with the departure of the Prophet ﷺ, and carries through to the martyrdom of Hazrat Imam Hussain (AS).
